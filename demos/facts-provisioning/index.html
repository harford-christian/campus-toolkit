— synced license assigned by Google org-unit
Licensing is fully automatic: once a Google account lands in an org-unit, M365 sync maps that OU to a license SKU — /Students → Microsoft A1, /Staff → Microsoft A3 — and assigns it. No one picks licenses by hand; the Google OU is the single control.
